Welcome to VolunMatch

Welcome to the Admin Dashboard of VolunMatch! As the administrator, you play a crucial role in facilitating volunteer engagement and matching suitable candidates for various events.

From this dashboard, you have access to a comprehensive overview of registered volunteers, allowing you to review their personal information, available days, preferred fields, and have experience or not.

With intuitive navigation and user-friendly controls, you can effortlessly set criteria for generating volunteers based on event days, fields of interest, and required expertise.

Whether you're planning a technology conference, cultural festival, or community outreach program, VolunMatch empowers you to find the right volunteers for the job, making a meaningful impact in your organization's initiatives.
